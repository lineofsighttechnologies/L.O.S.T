Who We Are

Line Of Sight Technologies is a Harare-based security solutions provider dedicated to safeguarding homes, businesses, and institutions across Zimbabwe. With a focus on CCTV systems, ICT networking, and biometric access control, we combine innovation with reliability to deliver peace of mind.

Our team of certified technicians and engineers brings deep expertise and a customer-first approach to every project. Whether you're upgrading your surveillance system or building a secure network from scratch, we tailor our solutions to meet your unique needs.
